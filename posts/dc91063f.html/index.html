Some Thoughts About My Fitness

2023-05-24 Thoughts

My body is getting stiffer and stiffer day by day.
It seems that no matter how much rest I take, my body and mind still feel like a rusty robot.
My bosses are just in their 30s, and they already got a round body, stomach pills, and medicine patches all over the office.
It’s not hard to imagine my 30s and my health end up like them if I keep working like this for a few more years.
I have a weak constitution and a slim physique. My body weight has remained at 50kg+ for a long time.
while I was a school student. I did many kinds of physical exercises such as hiking, sprinting, push-ups, pull-ups, etc.
Although I am thin, I still had enough explosive power and stamina.
However, when I reached high school age, physical exercise slowly became a rare thing, and my constitution is even weaker as a result.
I often consider that I am a Low-Energy person, I don’t consume much, therefore I have a low output.
If I do manual labor or brain work for too long, I get drained pretty quickly and require a relatively long time to recharge myself.
And I have realized that this status is quite toxic to my development.
For example, I would minimize my social activities, due to lack of energy.
I would rather stay home than go roaming outdoors, due to lack of energy.
And my brain would start to get numb and lose focus if I study something for a few hours.
In conclusion, I can’t do anything with such low energy.
Well, then how can I fix this? More exercise! Obviously.
But yet again, as a brain-work proletarian, I hit the wall of ‘lack of money and time’.
Being able to keep fit is indeed a privilege. because that indicated you have:
A job without overtime, and not too much intensity, or else you would not have the time and energy left for a workout.
Extra spending for supplements.
And also, ideally, the money for the gym. A weather-free, disciplined environment will affirmatively increase your motivation.
But of cause, I do realize I just need more exercise, not an eight-pack abs body that fits the petit-bourgeois aesthetics.
You can always find a way to work out for free if you want to but for now. lack of time and energy is a really big problem for me.
I have four days off per month, and I need to relax and recharge on those days, otherwise, I will be a zombie when I go back to work.
But anyway, I will do what I have to do, somehow.
